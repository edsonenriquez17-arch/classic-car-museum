The legacy

For over [XX] years, Jimmy has rescued, restored, and preserved automotive history in the heart of Lima.

From the World's Oldest Car to rare Porsche classics, each vehicle has an engine, a story, and a soul.

This is not a garage. This is a living Museum. And your experience starts here.
